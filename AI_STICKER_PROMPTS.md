# AI Generation Prompts for Transplant Sticker Pack

This document contains prompts for generative AI platforms to create additional high-quality stickers for the transplant sticker pack, focusing on liver, kidney, heart, and lung transplants.

## Visual Style Guidelines

Before using these prompts, note the existing sticker pack's visual characteristics:
- **Style**: Flat, simplified anatomical illustrations with modern aesthetic
- **Colors**: Medically accurate (heart: coral/red tones, liver: brown/tan, kidneys: brown with blue/red vessels, lungs: gray with white details)
- **Detail level**: Simplified but anatomically recognizable, suitable for small sticker format
- **Special elements**: Some stickers combine photorealistic hands with illustrated organs to show donation/gift concepts
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background, approximately 300x300 to 618x618 pixels
- **Purpose**: Educational, awareness-raising, supportive for transplant community

---

## Prompt 1: Transplant Success and Recovery Celebration Stickers

**Prompt for AI Platform:**

```
Create a set of 5 modern, flat-design medical sticker illustrations celebrating transplant success and recovery, focusing on liver, kidney, heart, and lung transplants. Each sticker should be 512x512 pixels, PNG format with transparent background.

Style requirements:
- Flat design with simplified anatomical accuracy
- Medical illustration aesthetic, not cartoon-like
- Appropriate organ colors: heart (coral pink and dark red), liver (brown/tan), kidneys (brown with blue/red vessels), lungs (gray with white bronchial detail)
- Clean, modern look suitable for iMessage stickers
- Positive, hopeful messaging

Stickers to create:

1. **Transplant Anniversary**: An anatomically accurate heart with a small gift bow or celebration ribbon on top, using coral and red gradient, with subtle sparkle/shine elements around it to suggest celebration

2. **Healthy New Kidney**: A healthy-looking kidney (brown/tan with blue and red vessels) with a subtle green checkmark or health indicator nearby, showing successful transplant outcome

3. **Breathing Easy**: A pair of lungs (gray with detailed white bronchial tree pattern) with small upward-flowing air streams or breath indicators (light blue wisps) suggesting restored breathing function

4. **Liver Recovery**: A healthy liver (brown/tan with subtle lobe definition) with a small green plant sprout or regeneration symbol, representing liver regeneration and recovery

5. **Second Chance at Life**: A glowing anatomical heart (coral/red tones) held in a photorealistic human hand (diverse skin tone) with subtle light rays emanating from the heart, symbolizing the gift of life

Design specifications:
- PNG format, transparent background
- 512x512 pixels
- Suitable for scaling down to 100x100px while maintaining clarity
- Medical accuracy in organ shapes
- Professional but warm and hopeful tone
```

## Prompt 2: Living Donation and Transplant Support Network Stickers

**Prompt for AI Platform:**

```
Create a set of 5 modern, medical-style sticker illustrations emphasizing living donation and the support network around transplants, focusing on liver, kidney, heart, and lung transplant themes. Each sticker should be 512x512 pixels, PNG format with transparent background.

Style requirements:
- Flat design medical illustration style
- Anatomically accurate but simplified for sticker format
- Color palette: heart (coral/red), liver (brown/tan), kidney (brown with colored vessels), lungs (gray with white detail)
- Mix of illustrated organs with photorealistic hand elements where appropriate
- Clean, professional appearance suitable for iMessage

Stickers to create:

1. **Living Liver Donation**: Two simplified liver illustrations side by side - one complete liver (representing donor) and one partial liver lobe (representing donated portion), connected by a small arrow or flow indicator, in brown/tan tones with subtle highlights showing the life-saving connection

2. **Kidney Transplant Journey**: An illustrated kidney (brown with blue/red vessels) with a simple dotted path or journey line from one side to the other, with a small heart symbol at the destination, representing the transplant journey from donor to recipient

3. **Heart Transplant Team**: Three stylized human silhouette outlines (simple, minimal) arranged in a supportive circle around an anatomical heart (coral/red tones) in the center, representing the medical team, family, and community support

4. **Lung Transplant Hope**: A pair of anatomically accurate lungs (gray with white bronchial tree detail) with a small dove or bird silhouette flying upward from the top, symbolizing renewed life and hope through lung transplantation

5. **Waiting with Hope**: An anatomical organ (heart, kidney, liver, or lungs - create artist's choice of most impactful) with a simple hourglass symbol beside it, using appropriate organ colors, representing the waiting list experience with hopeful undertones (include subtle warm glow around organ)

Design specifications:
- PNG format, transparent background  
- 512x512 pixels
- Maintain clarity when scaled to 100x100px
- Balance educational content with emotional appeal
- Respectful and dignified representation of transplant experience
- Suitable for diverse audiences including patients, donors, families, and medical professionals
```

## Usage Notes

1. **Platform Selection**: These prompts work best with advanced AI image generators like:
   - DALL-E 3
   - Midjourney v6
   - Adobe Firefly
   - Stable Diffusion XL with medical illustration models

2. **Iteration**: You may need to generate multiple versions and select the best results that match the existing sticker pack style

3. **Post-Processing**: 
   - Ensure transparent backgrounds
   - Verify appropriate resolution (300-618px recommended)
   - Test stickers at small sizes for legibility
   - Adjust colors if needed to match existing pack

4. **Testing**: Import generated stickers into Xcode project and test in Messages app simulator before finalizing

5. **Medical Accuracy**: Review generated organs with medical references to ensure anatomical appropriateness

## Integration Checklist

After generating stickers:
